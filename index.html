About Us

Our organization was found in 1999 with the goal of spreading the word of yoga to people all over the world. We believe yoga is a tool for inner transformation that can enhance the experience of life of each individual. Therefore we try to reach as many people we can and the lowest cost

Did you know?


  • Simple yoga practice takes only one hour of your day!

  • By practicing yoga on a daily basis you can make your body and mind more healthy.

  • Researches show that people who practice yoga tend to be more joyfull, healthy and live longer.

  • Yoga is the science of the body, and was first invented in india 2,000 years ago!

Classical Hatha Yoga


Hatha Yoga is the source of all yoga practices today. It was first initiated in India 2,000 years ago, by Adi Yogi, the first yogi ever.


Ever since it is being practiced by people around the world in order to make a better inner situation.


The word yoga means union, which means, when you practice yoga you want to unite with everything, to become one. Only then you can be trully joyfull by your own nature.
